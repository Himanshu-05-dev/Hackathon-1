General Finance FAQs

What is the difference between a savings account and a current account?

A savings account is for individuals to save money and earn interest, while a current account is designed for frequent transactions, typically used by businesses and professionals.

How do I create a personal budget?

List your income, track your expenses and ensure your spending doesn’t exceed your earnings.
